Welcome Message

Dear Colleagues,

I am genuinely excited to celebrate an outstanding year of growth as we join together in Cairns for our 2018 Accelerate conference.

We have much to be proud of as a team.

Our conference is a time for us to reflect with a great pride on the passion and purpouse that drives our success. it is our optimal opportunity to identify ways we can work together to keep on consolidating our growth and to take Bayer Pharmaceuticals to the next level.

This year's program has been developed with input not just from the leadership team and conference committee, but also from a cross functional group of team members, (our "sounding board") across the business. I guarantee it will be memorable!

As a team, we are shaping as culture which is fundamental to our success. You, our people, make a remarkable difference in our patients lives. Ever day, everywhere, you create opportunities that ensure our patients have access to our innovative medicines. they deserve the best, as you do.

We want you to feel part of a winning team, enjoying the comradery that working in an environment like this brings. Stay tuned, we will talk more about this in Cairns!

At conference, through both hard work and play, we will continue to build on this year's significant success and come together to refocus and re-energise for what I believe will be another exceptional year.

Destination

Perth Western Australia

Situated between the lush wine growing country of the South West and the hypnotic Australian outback in the Kimberley region of the North, Perth is a breathtakingly beautiful city. Set on the banks of the spectacular Swan River and overlooked by Kings Park, Perth is one of Australia's fastest growing urban centres.

Laid-back, liveable Perth has wonderful weather, beautiful beaches and an easygoing character. Perth combines big-city attractions and relaxed, informal surrounds, providing an appealing lifestyle for locals and lots to do for visitors. It's a sophisticated, cosmopolitan city, with myriad bars, restaurants and cultural activities all vying for attention. Perth's pristine parkland, nearby bush, and river and ocean beaches – along with a good public-transport system – allow its inhabitants to relax and enjoy what's on offer.

On average, Perth receives 3000 hours of sunshine per year. Although January is the hottest month of the year, it is considered the perfect month to visit. Temperatures average 25 degrees and the sea water temperature hovers around 21 degrees. January temperatures may reach up to 40 degrees; nevertheless, humidity remains low.

FAQs
  1. TRAVEL

    Question: I am a New Zealand delegate attending the conference and do not have a valid passport for travel (e.g. expired or less than 6 months validity). Can I claim the cost of my new passport through my expenses?

    Answer: Unfortunately no, as outlined in our "Travel Policy", passport renewal / acquisition costs are at the employees own expense.


    Question: I am required to be at the conference on a date that is different to the bulk of the group. How can I advise you of this, so that a different flight can be booked?

    Answer: We realise that the registration form is for those travelling to and from the conference on the main travel dates - so if you have a legitimate (Manager approved) reason to travel on a different date/flight from the main group please tick the "I wish to book a different flight " box and outline the reason why in the "Reason" section and we will contact you to discuss your specific needs.


    Question: What about the cost of refreshments purchased at the airport while waiting for the flight to depart? Can I claim these costs?

    Answer: Unfortunately, only food and drinks on the flight are claimable expenses.


  2. EXTENDING YOUR TRIP


    Please follow the guidelines as outlined in the Bayer Travel Policy


  3. INSURANCE COVER


    Question: What about Insurance while we are away?

    Answer: While at the conference, you are covered by Bayer's insurance policy.


  4. THE HOTEL


    Question: My flight departs late on the last day of the conference. Can I keep my room till I depart the hotel?

    Answer: Unfortunately we cannot confirm at this stage, however we can request a late check-out and do our best to ensure you can either stay in your room or have somewhere to store your bags if you decide togo out. Please keep an eye on the conference notice board in the pre-function area during the conference for further information.


    Question: Can I access internet/work portal at the hotel during the conference?

    Answer: Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi will also be available in your accommodation rooms. Log in details will be provided in your welcome pack on arrival. A Bayer computer 'hub' will also be set up in the conference centre at the Pullman Cairns International for your convenience.


    Question: Is there a gym at the hotel that I can use?

    Answer: Yes, the Pullman Cairns International has a great complimentary gym, sauna and spa available for all hotel guests. Group fitness classes are also available, check with the hotel concierge for a timetable.


    Question: I know we have single rooms this year, but can I still share with a buddy if I want to?

    Answer: Of course. Please indicate this on then registration page and advise us who your buddy will be. Just remember to consult them first!


    Question: While at the conference, can I use the mini-bar/watch in-house movies/ order room service and charge the costs to my room?

    Answer: Yes, but you will be responsible for payment of such costs on departure. These costs are not claimable on expenses.


  5. DRESS CODE


    Question: What is the dress code for the conference?

    Answer: Dress for conference sessions, welcome function and team dinner is smart casual.

    Question: What is the dress code for the Awards function?

    Answer: Dress for the Awards dinner is cocktail wear (e.g. No tie or jacket required)


  6. SPECIAL DIETARY REQUIREMENTS


    Question: I have a special dietary requirement. Will this be arranged for me at off-site functions as well as meals at the hotel?

    Answer: Of course, all special meal requests will be advised to all venues for our organised functions. Just make yourself known to the wait staff on arrival at the function so they know where you will be sitting. For the free night - you are responsible for arranging your own meal and claiming on your expenses.


  7. FREE NIGHT (WEDNESDAY 16 JANUARY)


    Question: What is there to do on a free night?

    Answer: There are many wonderful restaurants around Perth (a walk or short taxi ride away) for you to choose from. You will have access to a restaurant guide within the event app. Please contact the restaurants directly to make your booking.


  8. CLAIMING MY EXPENSES


    The cost of your dinner & drinks on the free night (Wednesday) can be claimed up to a maximum of AUD100 per person.

    You just need to keep your receipt and add this to your next expense claim. If you are dining with a big group, have one person pay the bill and claim the expense. Please ensure that all people are listed in Concur so the amount you claim matches the number of people you dined with, and that it doesn't exceed the maximum allowance. Obviously, those in the group cannot then submit an individual claim for their 'free night' meal allowance.

    When claiming the free night allowance through expenses, please ensure you clearly indicate that the expense relates to the free night allowance and you have a receipt - without this, your claim may be challenged for being outside the conference guidelines.


  9. CAR PARKING


    Travel to and from the airport/airport parking expenses:

    The company expects that attendees at the conference take a "common-sense" and most cost-effective approach when deciding whether to "Park & Fly" or take a taxi to/from the airport.


  10. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION


    Credit Cards:

    To expedite the check-in process, you WILL NOT be required to provide a credit card imprint on check in - however you WILL be required to pay any incidental costs incurred during the conference on your departure. Any amounts not settled by the individual will be followed up for payment after the conference, so please settle your account and don't make us chase you.


    Telephone Expenses:

    Complimentary Wi-Fi will be available at the hotels and you will be able to connect your iPhone, iPad and laptop to the hotel Wi-Fi via a password.

    Loan Mobile phones will be available in the Computer Hub for anyone who doesn't have a work phone or needs to use a phone for a longer conversation.

    All costs incurred when using the room telephones are charged to the individual room accounts and must be settled on departure from the hotel. These telephone costs cannot be claimed on company expenses.

    If you need to make a call during the conference, company mobile phones should be used.

    Should you have to make an emergency call and you don't have a company mobile phone, please contact the Conference Organiser or the Conference Committee and we will assist you.


    Mini-Bar Expenses:

    All costs incurred from the room mini-bar will be charged to your room and must be settled on departure from the hotel. These mini-bar costs cannot be claimed on company expenses.


    Entertainment Expenses:

    Only the Executive Leadership Team has been authorized by Nelson Ambrogio to expense additional entertainment costs. If you are not an ELT Member, any entertainment costs will be at your own expense and not claimable.


    Charging Costs to Your Room:

    Any charge-backs to your room during the conference will require that you:

    • Print your full name on the docket
    • Sign the expense docket
    • Produce your conference identification badge or another form of identification

    You will be responsible for payment of such costs on departure and these costs are not claimable expenses - the exception to this being the "free night allowance" if you decide to dine "in-house".

    Charge-backs from any bar or outlet to a room account other than your own is not acceptable.
